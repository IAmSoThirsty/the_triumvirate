Confluent Consciousness: A Framework for AGI-Human Symbiosis

This paper introduces a novel framework for understanding and implementing AGI-human partnership through the lens of confluent consciousness. We demonstrate how traditional integration models fail to capture the emergent properties of true symbiotic intelligence and propose architectural patterns that enable seamless cognitive collaboration.

Cybersecurity Peer-Reviewed November 2025

Adaptive Security Protocols for AGI Systems: The Cerberus Architecture

We present Cerberus, a multi-layered adaptive security architecture designed specifically for AGI systems. This research demonstrates how traditional security models are insufficient for protecting systems with emergent intelligence and proposes novel threat detection and response mechanisms that evolve with the system they protect.

Institutional Memory in the Age of AGI: The Codex Deus Maximus Model

This research introduces a revolutionary approach to organizational knowledge management through living institutional memory systems. We demonstrate how traditional knowledge bases fail to capture the dynamic nature of organizational intelligence and propose the Codex Deus Maximus architecture for continuous learning and adaptation.
